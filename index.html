Computer Animation, History and Future Technology

Published in International Journal of Trend in Research and Development (IJTRD)

This Article examine the several different views – historical through the modern computer generated and the future in computer animation. It covers a short history of animation with a detailed look with some example and emphasis the techniques used in the animation process. It also look at the future technologies, techniques and tricks used in animation and how computer changed the animation industry and what are the challenges and problems occur in computer animation.
